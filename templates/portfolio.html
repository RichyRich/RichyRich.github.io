Portfolio

A selection of work and personal projects I have completed over the years.
Much of my work has been for corporate clients and as such doesn't feature here, contact me for more details.

SmartLittleWeb.com

A website design and hosting service targeted at small to medium businesses in the UK.
Built on a Restlet/Freemarker stack and much of the front-end is jQuery.

Virgin Media Comments

Implementation and hosting of a restful commenting system for posts and stories on a popular consumer website.
Featured anti-spam/profanity mechanisms and full moderation capabilities. It was built using Restlet (again :) and a custom javascript API.
Credited for generating an additional 10 million PIs/month and live for approximately 5 years.

Bokonon (iPhone)

Bokonon was a casual iPhone game written mostly for fun and to gain experience using the cocos2d framework.
It was released as a free game on the AppStore and did quite well for a while, it's sadly no longer maintained.

Relaunch (OS X)

OS X app to reopen applications following a reboot. Sold well. OS X now has this feature built in. I wonder where they got the idea ;)

WifiMap UK (iPhone)

A wifi hotspot locator for the UK, allowing users to find and also rate wifi hotspots in their area. Geodata storage and backend admin site were built on Google AppEngine.

Noodleboard (OS X)

Noodleboard was an encrypted, multi-user notepad widget for OSX.

PSInvaders (Powershell)

Proud co-author of psinvaders, probably the first and finest game written exclusively for Powershell.
